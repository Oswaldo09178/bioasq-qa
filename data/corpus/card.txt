Applying for a FC Barcelona membership card begins with visiting the club’s official website and selecting the option to become a “socio.” You choose the membership category that fits your age group—adult, junior, or child—and review the applicable fees. Adults pay the full membership fee, while children and youth have reduced rates. Once the category is selected, you proceed by filling out the online registration form with your personal information, including identification details, a recent photograph, and payment or bank details.

After submitting your application and completing the payment, the club processes your request. If you applied online, your membership card is mailed to the address you provided, usually within a couple of weeks. Alternatively, you can complete the application in person at the club’s Supporter Services Office, where staff can assist with document verification and registration. Once approved, you officially become a Barcelona socio and can start enjoying the associated benefits, such as ticket discounts, voting rights (for eligible members), and access to club activities.
