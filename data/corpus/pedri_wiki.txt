Pedro González López, more commonly known as Pedri, is a Spanish professional footballer who plays as a midfielder for La Liga club Barcelona and the Spain national team. Considered one of the best players in the world, he is known for his ball control, dribbling, passing, playmaking, work rate and exceptional vision. Wikipedia
