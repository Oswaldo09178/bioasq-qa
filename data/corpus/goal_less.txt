The series of matches with goals from Barcelona have come to an end.

Today, the Catalan team suffered a 0-3 defeat to Chelsea in the fifth round of the Champions League group stage. This is the first time since 15 December 2024 that the Blaugrana have failed to score against their opponents, when they lost to Leganes (0-1). Since then, Barca had scored at least one goal in 53 consecutive matches.

Barcelona failed to score in a Champions League match for the first time since 7 November 2023, when they lost to Shakhtar (0-1). After that, the Catalans scored in 22 consecutive Champions League matches – their longest scoring streak in the competition since the one they had between 2009 and 2012 (29 matches with goals).
