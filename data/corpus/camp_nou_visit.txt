Visiting Camp Nou for a stadium tour begins with purchasing your ticket in advance through the official FC Barcelona ticketing platform. Booking online is recommended because it allows you to choose your preferred time slot and avoid queues on the day of your visit. Before you go, it’s helpful to check the opening hours and any restrictions, especially on match days when access to certain areas may be limited.

On the day of the visit, you simply head to the stadium’s tour entrance, where signs will direct you to the museum and tour access points. Once there, you present your digital ticket—usually on your phone—and a valid ID. After entry, you can enjoy the full experience, which typically includes the Barça Museum, immersive historical exhibits, and panoramic views of the stadium. Depending on availability, you may also explore special add-ons such as guided tours, virtual reality experiences, or premium “player experience” packages that offer deeper access behind the scenes.
