Fútbol Club Barcelona has closed the 2025/26 summer transfer window with the departure of up to 10 players. With the departure of Héctor Fort on loan to Elche CF on the last day of the market, the Azulgrana team has finally added ten departures to its squad.

Four players have done so on loan for one season, three have been transferred and another three agreed to end their relationship with a definitive contract termination. In economic terms, the 10 departures have left revenues of 23 million in the Blaugrana coffers and a salary saving of close to 37 million euros.

The ten players who have left FC Barcelona in the 2025/26 market
03/06/2025: Álex Valle (transfer to Como for 6 M€)
09/06/2025: Lenglet (contract termination, saving of around 15 M€)
29/06/2025: Ander Astralaga (loan)
01/07/2025: Ansu Fati (loan to Mónaco, saving of 7 M€)
14/07/2025: Pablo Torre (transfer to Mallorca for 5 M€)
25/07/2025: Pau Víctor (transfer to Braga for 12+3 M€)
09/08/2025: Í. Martínez (contract termination, saving of 8.4 M€)
27/08/2025: Iñaki Peña (loan to Elche, saving 3 M€)
28/08/2025: Oriol Romeu (contract termination, saving 3.5 M€)
31/08/2025: Héctor Fort (loan to Elche)
