Age / Eligibility

Candidates are generally between 6 and 18 years old — La Masia accepts young players from an early age (often from as young as 6). 

Younger categories (pre‑Benjamin, Benjamin, etc.) target the very early developmental stages, while older youth categories prepare players for potential professional progression. 

🎯 Talent, Skills & Potential

Prospective players must show strong technical skills, good footballing intelligence/tactical awareness, and appropriate physical potential — the club values technique, vision, and a style that matches Barça’s philosophy. 
La Masia

More than raw talent: scouts and coaches also look for attitude, discipline, work ethic, humility, teamwork, and commitment to improvement — character and mentality matter a lot. 

🔎 Recruitment & Selection Process

Entry typically involves being scouted (either via local clubs, regional networks or via official trials), rather than open‑to‑all walk‑in tryouts — La Masia’s scouting network identifies promising players. 

Once identified, a young player may undergo trials or assessments, sometimes involving drills, match simulations and evaluations of both skill and character.
