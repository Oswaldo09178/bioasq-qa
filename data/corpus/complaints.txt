Filing an official complaint or inquiry with FC Barcelona involves a structured process to ensure your message reaches the appropriate department. The first step is to determine the nature of your concern. Complaints or inquiries can relate to ticketing, membership, merchandise, stadium experiences, academy issues, or general club operations. Knowing the category of your concern will help direct your request to the correct department, improving response time and accuracy.

Once you have identified the subject of your complaint, visit the official FC Barcelona website and locate the “Contact” or “Customer Service” section. Here, you will usually find a form specifically designed for inquiries and complaints. You need to provide your personal details, such as name, email, and membership number if applicable, along with a clear and concise description of your issue. Attaching relevant documents, screenshots, or receipts can strengthen your case and provide clarity for the staff handling your complaint.

After submitting the form, you will receive an acknowledgment confirming that your complaint has been received. The club’s relevant department will review the information and investigate the matter as necessary. Depending on the complexity of the complaint, response times can vary, and you may be contacted for additional information. Once the investigation is complete, FC Barcelona will provide a formal response or resolution, which may include corrective actions, explanations, or guidance on next steps. Following up politely after a reasonable period can help ensure your issue is addressed if no response has been received.
