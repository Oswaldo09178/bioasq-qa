Upgrading or modifying an existing FC Barcelona membership involves a few administrative steps that must be completed directly through the club’s official channels. The first step is to log into your personal member account on FC Barcelona’s official website using your member credentials. Once logged in, you can access the “Membership” or “Socis” section, where the club provides options for updating personal details, modifying payment methods, or requesting changes such as switching to a different membership category. Before submitting any modification, it is important to review the requirements and conditions associated with the specific change you want to make, since some upgrades may require additional documentation or eligibility confirmation.

After identifying the exact modification you want, you must submit a formal request through the online platform or by contacting the club’s Membership Office. This can be done via the official contact form, email, or by visiting the office in person if you are in Barcelona. The club may request supporting documents, especially for changes related to age categories, family memberships, or status upgrades. Once the request is submitted, the membership team reviews it and provides confirmation when the change is approved. Any applicable fees must then be paid to finalize the modification. After processing, the club updates your membership profile, and if needed, sends a new membership card reflecting your updated status.
