Paris Saint-Germain’s Ousmane Dembele and Barcelona’s Aitana Bonmati have won the top prizes at the 2025 Ballon d’Or awards ceremony in Paris, France.

The first 20 years of Ousmane Dembele's life — and therefore, career — were very linear. Coming through at little-known French clubs before moving to Rennes of Ligue 1, he was soon taken very seriously by European football fans.

Impressive performances there brought a move to Borussia Dortmund and little over a year later, Barcelona made him the second most expensive player of all-time.

His career trajectory stalled, and then started to drop, in Catalonia and his underwhelming time at Camp Nou ended in 2023 when he returned to France with PSG.

And the rest, as they say, is history. Last season shot Dembele into a stratosphere that no Barca fan expected and it brought PSG the prize they were so desperate to win; the Champions League.

Dembele and his team-mates were 90 minutes away from the perfect season until they were beaten by Chelsea in the Club World Cup final.
