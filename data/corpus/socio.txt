Here are the main steps to become a member (socio) of FC Barcelona — i.e., to join as a “socio.” 


1. Choose the right membership type (age/category)

Decide which category applies to you — there are different options depending on your age: children (0–5 years), juniors (6–14 years), or adult (15+). 

Membership costs vary accordingly: for example, adult membership fee is about €219 (for the current period). 

2. Complete the registration form (online or in person)

You can register online via the club’s official “Become a Member / Hacerse Socio” portal. 

Alternatively, you can go in person to the club’s Supporter Services Office (OAB at Camp Nou) — by appointment — to register physically. 

3. Provide required documentation and pay the fee

For adults: usually requires ID/passport, a photograph, and bank account/payment details for the membership fee. 

For registering a minor (child / junior): a parent or legal guardian must apply, and provide among others: their own ID, the child's family‑book (or proof of parenthood), the child’s photo, and bank/payment details. 

Payment is typically by bank card (or as directed), and once approved, the membership will be valid until December 31 of the current cycle. 

4. Wait for confirmation and receive your membership card

After submitting the application and paying the fee, you will receive a confirmation. For online applications, the membership card (socio‑card) is usually delivered to the address you provided. Estimated time is about two weeks. 

As of recent seasons, the club issues a digital card / digital membership card — physical cards are optional and generally provided on request (or to certain groups like seniors).
